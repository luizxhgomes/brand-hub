Design Notes

  • Concept 1 "O Arquiteto" -- The L and H share a vertical stroke, symbolizing the connection point where the architect bridges all areas. Cleanest option, strongest at small sizes. Most versatile for favicon and app icon use.
  • Concept 2 "O Construtor" -- 10 modular blocks build the LH letterform on a grid. References building systems piece by piece, from the ground up. The grid structure evokes data dashboards and operational frameworks.
  • Concept 3 "O Sistema" -- 7 primary nodes (the 7 integrated areas) connected by lines form the LH. The dashed bridge between L and H represents integration across departments. Most narrative-rich, best for presentations and brand storytelling.
  • Color strategy: Solid #2563EB is the primary mark. The blue-to-cyan gradient is used for digital/marketing contexts where more visual energy is appropriate. Both pass WCAG AA contrast on dark (#0A0E1A) and light (#F8FAFC) backgrounds.
  • Favicon recommendation: Concept 1 renders most clearly at 16px due to its bold rectangular construction with minimal detail.
