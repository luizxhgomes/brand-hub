Design Notes -- v2 Curved & Organic Direction

  • Concept A "Ascensao" -- Two interlocking crescents built entirely with cubic bezier curves. The left crescent rises from bottom-left, the right descends from top-right. Together they create a dynamic yin-yang movement that subtly suggests "LH" in the negative space between the forms. The upward visual energy represents rising from the Complexo da Mare to the tech elite. Most versatile concept -- works equally well as favicon and at large scale.
  • Concept B "Fluxo Continuo" -- A single unbroken stroke using quadratic and cubic bezier curves. The line never breaks, representing persistence and resilience -- what never stops, never fails. The flowing path traces an abstract "LH" as one continuous gesture, like a personal signature. The rounded stroke-linecap adds warmth and authenticity. Best concept for personal branding contexts where approachability matters.
  • Concept C "Estrela Persistente" -- A 4-pointed star with deliberately asymmetric proportions: the top point reaches higher (ambition, persistence) while the bottom is shorter but grounded (solid foundations, origin). All star edges use smooth cubic bezier curves for an organic feel rather than sharp geometric points. Contained in a subtle circle inspired by CryptoGen's clean containment approach. Strongest concept for app icons and social media profile images.
  • Curve philosophy: All three concepts use exclusively cubic (C) and quadratic (Q) bezier commands in SVG paths. No rectangles, straight lines, or geometric primitives. Every edge flows and breathes. The visual language is fluid, organic, and alive -- reflecting authenticity over rigidity.
  • Color strategy: Base form uses single flat fill #2563EB (no gradients in primary version). The gradient variant applies the brand gradient (#2563EB to #06B6D4) for digital contexts where more visual energy is needed. Both pass WCAG AA contrast ratios on dark (#0A0E1A) and light (#F8FAFC) backgrounds.
  • Scalability: All concepts use viewBox="0 0 64 64" for compact, resolution-independent rendering. Tested at 128px (hero), 64px (navigation), 32px (toolbar), and 16px (favicon). Concept A and C maintain clarity at 16px. Concept B may benefit from slightly thicker strokes at the smallest size.
